This is not a prediction about your week. It is the thing your most observant friend would say if she had read your chart and decided to be honest about it.

Find your sign below. If you read someone else's sign first because it described them perfectly, that tracks. Stay anyway.

## Aries

You do not need permission to slow down, but you keep waiting for it anyway. Nobody is going to hand you a reason to rest. Take one. The thing you are racing toward will still be there in an hour.

## Taurus

Comfort and avoidance have started looking identical to you, and that is worth noticing. Not everything that feels safe is actually serving you anymore. Some of it is just familiar.

## Gemini

You already know the answer. You are asking five more people because you want someone else to be responsible for the outcome. Trust the version of you that decided this three days ago.

## Cancer

Caring for everyone else is not the same as being close to anyone, including yourself. Let someone show up for you this month without immediately finding a way to deserve it first.

## Leo

The applause was never actually the point, even though it has started to feel that way. The thing you built before anyone was watching is still the part worth being proud of.

## Virgo

You are allowed to leave something imperfect and still call it finished. Not every project needs the extra round of polish you keep finding reasons to add.

## Libra

Indecision has started costing you more than a wrong choice ever would. Pick the option that scares you slightly less. That is usually the real answer underneath all the deliberating.

## Scorpio

You read people accurately and then talk yourself out of trusting your own read. Stop doing that. Your instincts about this particular situation were correct the first time.

## Sagittarius

Freedom does not have to mean distance from everyone who loves you. You can stay and still feel like yourself. The two are not actually in conflict, even though it has felt that way before.

## Capricorn

Rest is not a reward you earn after the work is finished, because the work is never finished. You get to rest now, mid-list, unfinished and all.

## Aquarius

Being different does not require being unreachable. Let someone close enough to actually know the parts of you that are not on display.

## Pisces

Feeling everything is not the same as drowning in it, even on the days it is hard to tell the difference. You are allowed to feel this much and still be okay.

Take what fits, leave what does not. The stars are a starting point, not a verdict. The rest is still up to you.
